Strategic Business Card Ideas

Chance Smith

When I created my new business cards the experience helped me think of how I can be more strategic next time. Maybe these will spark some ideas for your next business cards too.

  • give tips in your industry
  • promo a book or product
  • give a CTA to a landing page
  • share a link to a page you are A/B testing
  • offer special for filling out a survey (link to survey)
  • show a mini case study
  • share details of a class or workshop you offer
